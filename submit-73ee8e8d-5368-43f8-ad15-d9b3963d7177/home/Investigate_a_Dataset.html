Introduction¶

Exploring the tmdb_movie dataset to find certain factors and infer the factors that may affect the profit margins made on each of the movie. the process starts with noticing bits and pieces of the dataset and cleaning the dataset, removing the redundant cells and records and removing null valuee for further inspection. Getting the correct and the trimmed dataset on which the further investigation has to be conducted, laying the foundation on actual exploratory analysis. Here, the exploratory study involves noting the factors mainly the director and the year of release affecting the profitability of the movie. A look over these attributes and how they affect the profit margins. Secondly, a venture into getting correct figures for runtime, budget,revenue and other factors, but not from the business prospective.Looking out for factors that affect the trends and deviations in profit made out on movies.

What I am trying to Do? Get enough elemnts into the picture so that we can understand what may have worked for a particular movie so that it had a high turnover. Analytical findings on the directors, the budget,revenue, profit margins of movies, to make a note of the directors, the cast, the year, the profitability margins for the movie and understanding what may have worked for a highly grossed movie or what may not have worked for a low turnover movie so that next time the production company wants to make a movie that runs in the boxoffice that can use these findings as metrics - as in hire the cast/director of movies that had higher profitable margins or make movies on the same genres as these movies. Check if the year that had the most profitable movie, if there was some events or something that may have triggered the sale of the movies.
